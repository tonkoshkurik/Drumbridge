# Drumbridge

**Sample manager for Teenage Engineering EP-133 K.O. II, EP-1320 Medieval, and EP-40 RIDDIM.**

A native iOS / iPadOS app that connects to your EP groove box over USB-C and gives you everything the device's small screen can't: a full sample browser, project playback, backup & restore, and Ableton / MIDI / stems export — all offline, no account, no cloud.

> Drumbridge is an independent project. Not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Teenage Engineering AB. All product names are trademarks of their respective owners and are used solely to describe compatibility.

---

## Features

- **Sample management** — browse all 999 slots, upload with auto-resampling to the device's native 46875 Hz, download as WAV, batch operations
- **Project playback** — load any project, play it back in-app with scenes, 12-type fader automation, FX send/return, sidechain, master compressor
- **Backup & restore** — full `.ppak` backups with cross-device conversion (EP-133 ↔ EP-40)
- **Export** — Ableton Live `.als`, MIDI Type 1, per-pad stem WAVs with all automation and FX baked in
- **Sample editor** — trim, chop, auto-slice, zoom, save back to slot
- **Auto-sampler** — chromatic one-pass capture from external synths over MIDI
- **PO-32 drum synth** — built-in for on-device sound design
- **Lo-fi presets** — EP native, hi-fi, SP-1200, SP-1201, SK-1, lo-fi with 8-bit quantization
